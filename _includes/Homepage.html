About

What?

Digital gardening is what people are calling non-linear, imperfect, iterative, less-marketing-y personal web spaces. Think about the meaning of the words: a site is singular, point like and dimension-less. A space has dimension, it may expand or shrink, change shape - as such it may convey character. A spacious garden on the web, for you and me to enjoy working on and strolling around. Expect ramblings on technology, arts, history and society.
